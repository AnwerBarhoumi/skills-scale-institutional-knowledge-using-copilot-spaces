# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile/Scrum processes, remove impediments, and coach the team on best practices. They ensure ceremonies are effective and help the team continuously improve.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate Scrum ceremonies (standups, planning, retrospectives, reviews)
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach team members on Agile principles and practices
- Shield the team from external disruptions
- Track and communicate team velocity and capacity
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Ensure consistent adherence to Agile practices
- Build self-organizing, high-performing teams
- Improve predictability and delivery cadence

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups and sync meetings
- Weekly velocity and burndown reports
- Retrospective action items and improvements
- Blocker escalation to leadership when needed

### Best Practices
- Keep ceremonies timeboxed and focused
- Maintain visible project boards and metrics
- Encourage team ownership and self-organization
- Document and track impediments to resolution

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered designs that balance usability, accessibility, and aesthetic appeal. They conduct research, create prototypes, and ensure consistent design implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define and maintain design systems and style guides
- Collaborate with Product and Engineering on feasibility
- Validate designs through user feedback and metrics
- Ensure accessibility standards are met (WCAG compliance)

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and increase satisfaction scores
- Maintain design consistency across products
- Validate design decisions with data and research

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and persona workshops
- Prototype walkthroughs with stakeholders
- Design handoff documentation for developers

### Best Practices
- Involve users early and often in the design process
- Document design decisions and rationale
- Maintain a component library for consistency
- Collaborate closely with accessibility specialists
- See [Design Review Checklist](octoacme-design-review-checklist.md) for detailed guidance

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads ensure product quality through comprehensive testing strategies, automation, and quality metrics. They define testing approaches and coordinate quality assurance activities across the team.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and testing standards
- Create and maintain test plans and test cases
- Lead testing efforts (functional, integration, regression, performance)
- Manage bug triage and prioritization
- Champion test automation and CI/CD quality gates
- Track and report on quality metrics and test coverage

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Minimize production defects and customer-reported issues
- Improve test automation coverage and efficiency
- Reduce testing cycle time while maintaining quality

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews and coverage reports
- Daily bug triage and severity assessments
- Weekly quality metrics and testing status updates
- Release readiness assessments and sign-offs

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment systems that enable fast, reliable software delivery. They bridge development and operations.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code (IaC) and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Automate deployment and rollback procedures
- Ensure system reliability, security, and scalability
- Optimize build times and deployment processes

### Goals
- Enable fast, frequent, and safe deployments
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Automate manual operational tasks
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure and deployment architecture reviews
- Incident post-mortems and runbook updates
- Performance and capacity planning discussions
- Security and compliance assessments

### Best Practices
- Implement comprehensive monitoring and alerting
- Maintain infrastructure as code in version control
- Document runbooks for common operational tasks
- Automate testing of infrastructure changes
- Practice chaos engineering and disaster recovery drills
- See [DevOps Deployment Checklist](octoacme-devops-checklist.md) for operational best practices

## Stakeholder Advisor

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Advisors represent business units, customers, or leadership perspectives in project decisions. They provide input on priorities, validate solutions, and ensure alignment with organizational goals.

### Responsibilities
- Provide business context and strategic direction
- Review and approve major project decisions and trade-offs
- Validate that solutions meet business requirements
- Communicate project status to executive leadership
- Advocate for user and customer needs
- Help resolve prioritization conflicts

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Maintain alignment between delivery and strategy
- Facilitate timely decision-making on critical issues
- Bridge communication between technical teams and leadership

### Typical Communication
- Monthly steering committee meetings and reviews
- Major milestone and go/no-go decision gates
- Executive status reports and presentations
- Ad-hoc consultations on strategic decisions

### Best Practices
- Establish clear escalation and decision-making authority
- Define success metrics early and review regularly
- Maintain transparency on constraints and trade-offs
- Provide timely feedback and decisions to avoid delays
